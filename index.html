why HBO-ICT?

Working with computers is one of the things I enjoy doing. There is no logical reasoning behind it. I just really enjoy it for what it is. Computers are very intricate machines, making it able to do something simple like showing a letter or calculating 1+1 is an enormous task. At least it used to be. Computers can do more these days than being a big calculator. I want to know just how much they can do. I'm looking forward to learning about all the secrets of this machine. And if possible, contribute to it's development. Being able to help advance this technology in any way whatsoever would be a dream come true for me.

DIY chicken burger step by step

  1. take the bottom of the bread
  2. sread some mayo on it
  3. put a bit of cabbage on it
  4. put three slices of cucumber ontop
  5. the burger ofcourse
  6. two slices of tomato on top
  7. some cooked onions on the tomatoes
  8. put a bit of spicy samurai sauce on it
  9. put half a slice of young gouda cheese on top
  10. spread some mayo on the top of the bun, just like the bottom
  11. microwave it enough for the cheese to get melted a bit
  12. eat and enjoy!
