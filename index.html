why HBO-ICT?

Working with computers is one of the joys in life for me. There is no logical reasoning behind it. I just really enjoy it for what it is. Computers are very intricate machines, making it able to do something simple like showing a letter or calculating 1+1 is an enormous task. At least it used to be. Computers can do more these days than being a big calculator. I want to know just how much they can do. I'm looking forward to learning about all the secrets of this machine. And if possible, contribute to it's development. Being able to help advance this technology in any way whatsoever would be a dream come true for me.
