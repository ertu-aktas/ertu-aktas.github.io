DIY chicken burger step by step

  1. Take the bottom of the bread.
  2. Spread some mayo on it.
  3. Put a bit of cabbage on it.
  4. Put three slices of cucumber on top.
  5. Take the burger and put the cheese on top.
  6. Microwave only the burger with the cheese on top, enough for the cheese to get melted a bit.
  7. Put it back on the burger.
  8. Two slices of tomato on top.
  9. Some cooked onions on the tomatoes.
  10. Put a bit of spicy samurai sauce on it.
  11. Spread some mayo on the top of the bun, just like the bottom.
  12. Put the top of the bread on top.
  13. eat and enjoy!
