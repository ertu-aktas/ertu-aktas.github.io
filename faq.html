Frequenlty Asked Questions

  • How to print a document from your laptop at HZ?

-        First to go to the online HZ print webpage. log into your account and upload all          necessary files you want to print. Go to the printer, scan your card in it, choose          printing option and hit print.

  • How to scan a document and send it to your laptop?

-        The proper instructions are located here

  • How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

-        Go to this link, log in and make the desired purchase. You can't make a          purchase without logging in.

  • What to do if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

-        You need to reveal you HZ pass at the barriers so you can get through and into          the parking lot. Due to the expansion of the building, the parking spaces are          limited and reserved only for those who truly need it.

  • How to book a project space for study and/or meetings?

-         First, either go to the HZ portal or this link. From here on out you need to           follow the following path:

          Make a reservation > Reserve meeting room or Project Group room >           Reserve a room

          Now all you have to do is locate the proper location and drag the hours on the           desired row.

  • Where can you find the latest instructions on what to do when you have COVID?

-        You can find the answer to all your COVID related questions on this page.
