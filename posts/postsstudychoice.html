Questions about my study choice

  • What did you yourself do in deciding if ICT is the right choice of study for you?

I mostly talked to people who are already active in the field. Besides that I took free courses to get a taste of what writing a web page or a program is like. It's fun to make one.

  • Why did you choose ICT? what part of the deciding process made you choose this study?

Most notable was the talking to others who are already working in the field. Especially two of my friends and my brother in law. My two friends have also studied ICT at HZ Middelburg. they have very positive experiences and they have enjoyed their time at the school. They really like coding and think it's fun. I too have learned in these last two weeks how fun coding can be. On the other side also how frustrating, but that makes solving the problems all the more fun when I do finally get it right.
