Feedback

  • Motivation

When it comes to motivation you mention you enjoy working with computers, what exactly do you mean, can you make it more specific? what exactly do you like about it? what type of work do you refer to? what makes it interesting for your motivation to study ICT that ICT is at the front of technological advancements?

  • Workfield

Not knowing what you want to do after completing the study or what you like about ICT is known to be one of the reasons why students drop out or do not succeed. So take these first two weeks of introduction to find out more about the work field and try to come up with an answer of what piques your interest in the IT field from what you have found out about it so far.

  • SWOT

Where does the worrying come from do you think? is it not having enough information or understanding which becomes frustrating? Insecurity for being good enough? Finding it difficult to make decisions? never satisfied with what you achieve? (perfectionism) or fearing the unknown? when you know the reason behind your behavior you can find and learn the coping skills to aply worrying (I would call it risk management in that case) when you need it to steer away from danger. And to stop worrying when it holds you back from learning, joy, and new experiences you need for professional growth.
